Turkiye is known as the bridge between Europe and Asia. This is because Turkiye is bordered by countries from both continents including Armenia, Georgia, Iran, Azerbaijan, Bulgaria, Greece, Iraq and Syria. So it is no wonder that Turkey boasts a rich culture and history.

Although meat-based foods such as kebabs are common in Turkish cuisine abroad, meals in Turkey largely center around rice, vegetables, and bread.
