ABOUT ME

My name is Sedat Cavdar, and I bring over 15 years of experience as an English Teacher. Additionally, I am the author of supplementary English books designed for elementary and secondary school students. Currently, I serve as the Newcomer Manager at Calgary YMCA, where we run the LINC Program (Language Instruction for Newcomers to Canada).

I am thrilled to have successfully developed this website despite lacking an IT background. I want to express my gratitude to my Professor, for providing clear instructions and informative sessions on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which greatly contributed to my achievement.
